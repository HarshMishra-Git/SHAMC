Excellence in Ayurvedic Healthcare

Swati Hospital and Ayurvedic Medical College is a prominent institution dedicated to advancing healthcare and education in the field of Ayurveda. Established in 2018 in Muzaffarnagar, Uttar Pradesh, our institution stands as a bridge between ancient wisdom and modern healthcare needs. Located in a tranquil environment at Budhana Road Tawli, our hospital offers a comprehensive range of holistic treatments that focus on natural healing methods rooted in ancient Indian practices.

Our facility is equipped with state-of-the-art laboratories, a well-stocked library containing ancient texts and contemporary research materials, and a lush herbal garden showcasing a diversity of medicinal plants. We maintain a strong commitment to traditional healing methodologies while embracing modern advancements, ensuring optimal patient care and academic excellence.

Affiliated with Mahayogi Guru Gorakhnath AYUSH University and approved by the National Commission for Indian System of Medicine (NCISM), we offer quality medical education under the supervision of highly qualified faculty with state-of-the-art infrastructure.

Ayurvedic Treatments

Comprehensive holistic healing solutions rooted in 5000 years of ancient wisdom

The Science of Life - Ayurveda

Ayurveda is an ancient knowledge cultivated by the sages of India. 'Ayur' means life and 'Veda' means science. Thus, the term 'Ayurveda' means 'science of life'. The theories and notions of Ayurveda have been practiced since the past 5000 years.

It recommends methods for appropriate living and longevity, including instructions to maintain good health as well as dealing with illness through yoga, treatments, herbal medicines, correct diet and lifestyle changes.

At Swati Hospital, we combine traditional Ayurvedic wisdom with modern medical facilities to provide comprehensive healthcare solutions that address the root cause of ailments rather than just treating symptoms.

Panchakarma

Complete detoxification therapy

Panchakarma Therapy

A comprehensive detoxification and rejuvenation treatment that includes five therapeutic procedures to cleanse the body of toxins and restore natural balance.

  • Vamana (Therapeutic vomiting)
  • Virechana (Purgation therapy)
  • Basti (Medicated enemas)
  • Nasya (Nasal administration)
  • Raktamokshana (Bloodletting)

Abhyanga

Therapeutic oil massage

Abhyanga Massage

A full-body therapeutic oil massage that improves circulation, relieves stress, and promotes overall wellness through the use of medicated oils.

  • Skin nourishment
  • Better sleep quality

Shirodhara

Continuous oil pouring therapy

Shirodhara Treatment

A deeply relaxing treatment where warm medicated oil is continuously poured over the forehead to calm the nervous system and promote mental clarity.

  • Mental relaxation and clarity
  • Stress and anxiety relief

  • Headache and migraine relief
  • Enhanced concentration

Rasayana

Rejuvenation therapy

Rasayana Therapy

Rejuvenation and anti-aging treatments using specialized herbs and therapies to enhance vitality, immunity, and longevity.

Basti

Medicated enema therapy

Basti Therapy

Basti in Ayurveda is considered to be the most effective and important therapy in panchakarma for removing toxins from the body. Medical fluid is introduced into the rectum for better delivery to the colon.

  • Improved digestion

  • Joint and muscle health
  • Hormonal balance

Admission Process

1

NEET Qualification

Candidates must qualify for the NEET UG entrance exam conducted by NTA with minimum required percentile.

2

UP AYUSH Counselling

Participate in Uttar Pradesh Ayush NEET Counselling conducted by UP State Ayush Society, Government of Uttar Pradesh.

3

Document Verification

Submit required documents including NEET scorecard, 10+2 certificates, and other necessary documents for verification.

4

Seat Allotment

Based on NEET rank and counselling preferences, seats are allotted. Report to college with provisional allotment letter.

Documents Required

NEET Admit Card & Scorecard
Class 10 & 12 Certificates
ID Proof (Aadhar/PAN/Passport)
Passport Size Photographs
